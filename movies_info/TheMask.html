The Mask (1994)

The Mask is a 1994 American superhero comedy film directed by Chuck Russell and produced by Bob Engelman.
The first installment in The Mask franchise, it stars Jim Carrey in the title role. Carrey plays Stanley Ipkiss,
a hapless, everyday bank clerk who finds a magical wooden green mask that transforms him into The Mask,
a green-faced troublemaker with the ability to cartoonishly alter himself and his surroundings at will.
