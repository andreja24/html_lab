About a Boy (2002)

About a Boy is a 2002 romantic comedy-drama film directed by Paul Weitz and Chris Weitz, who co-wrote
the screenplay with Peter Hedges. It is an adaptation of the 1998 novel of the same name by Nick Hornby.
The film stars Hugh Grant, Nicholas Hoult, Toni Collette, and Rachel Weisz. The film at times uses double
voice-over narration, when the audience hears alternately Will's and Marcus's thoughts.
