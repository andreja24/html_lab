Dievų Miškas (1945)

The Forest of the Gods is a memoir written in 1945 by a Lithuanian writer and poet Balys Sruoga.
It reflects the writer's experiences in the Nazi concentration camp, to which B. Sruoga was sent
in 1943. The memoir is full of irony and mockery of the order of the camps, the treatment of
the prisoners, and indeed of the entire current situation.
